Overview

This viewer runs the separated globe-earth runtime with astronomy, routes, constellations, and rocket simulation in one place.

Bundled globe texture: ../assets/nasa-blue-marble-5400x2700.jpg. Edit globe/modules/* to develop the detached globe runtime without touching the flat app.

  1. Boot the detached globe viewer directly from globe/index.html.
  2. Implement globe scene and UI logic under globe/modules/*.
  3. Reuse shared assets from ../assets/* while keeping runtime code isolated.

Astronomy

Sync the sun and moon to live time, preview alternate dates, inspect eclipse states, and tune orbit or camera presentation.

Routes

Replay offline flight legs, adjust playback speed, and inspect aircraft, duration, and progress metadata.

Constellations

Toggle the sky layer, browse named constellations, and inspect direction, RA, declination, and hemisphere data.

Rockets

Launch scripted rockets from real-world spaceports and inspect live telemetry in the rocket panel.

Navigation & Camera

Drag to orbit the camera, use the wheel to zoom, reset to the default framing at any time, and keep the active section while switching layouts.

First Person

Enable Walker View to explore from the surface. Use WASD or arrow keys to move and drag to look around.
